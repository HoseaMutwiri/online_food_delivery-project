# Online Food Delivery - Business Analytics

**SQL + Power BI** | End-to-End Data Analysis Project

---
## 📋 Problem Statement

The online food delivery industry is highly competitive. Companies face challenges in understanding revenue drivers, customer preferences, delivery performance, and the effectiveness of discounts. 

This project was designed to answer critical business questions and provide actionable recommendations through data-driven insights.

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Measure overall revenue and order performance
- Identify top performing cities, cuisines, and customers
- Analyse delivery efficiency and late deliveries
- Understand customer payment behaviour and cuisine preferences
- Track monthly revenue trends
- Provide an interactive dashboard for stakeholders

## 📊 Dataset Overview

- **Database**: PostgreSQL (`online_p` schema)
- **Tables**: `customers`, `restaurant`, `orders`, `order_items`, `delivery_agents`

The dataset contains transactional records including order amount, delivery time, payment method, customer details, and restaurant information.

## 🛠️ Methodology & Tools

- **SQL Analysis**: Exploratory analysis, Customer segmentation, Performance metrics, Advanced SQL (CTEs, Window Functions, Views, Stored Procedures, Triggers)
- **Visualization Tool**: Microsoft Power BI
- **Techniques**: Data modelling, DAX measures, Interactive slicers, Professional dashboard design

---

## 📈 Key Insights

### KPI Summary
- **Total Revenue**: 1.34 Million
- **Total Orders**: 1,001
- **Total Customers**: 296
- **Average Delivery Time**: 40.22 minutes

### Major Observations
- Mumbai is the highest revenue-generating city, followed by Chennai and Kolkata.
- Most popular cuisines: South Indian, Indian, Italian, Fast Food, Chinese, Desserts
- UPI and Card payments dominate.
- Revenue shows a declining trend from March to September.
- Male customers contribute slightly higher revenue than female customers.

---

## 🔑 Key Findings

1. Mumbai leads significantly in revenue contribution.
2. South Indian and Indian cuisines are the most popular.
3. Digital payments (UPI & Card) form the majority of transactions.
4. Average delivery time of 40.22 minutes indicates scope for improvement.
5. Revenue has been declining month-on-month urgent action required.
6. A small group of high-value customers drives a large portion of total revenue.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Prioritize marketing and expansion in high-performing cities (Mumbai & Chennai).
- Create targeted offers and promotions for South Indian & Indian cuisines.
- Focus on reducing average delivery time through better logistics and agent incentives.
- Promote UPI & Card payments with cashback offers.
- Launch loyalty and retention programs for high-value customers.
- Design seasonal and festive campaigns to reverse the declining revenue trend.
